Common Questions
When does 2027 registration open? ▾
Registration for the 2027 event opens later this year. Join the mailing list using the form to the left and you'll be the first to know when spots become available.
How do I register — is it through this site? ▾
Registration is handled through RunReg, our official registration platform. You can access it via the Register page. Spots are limited and fill quickly.
I'm interested in sponsoring the 2027 event. ▾
We'd love to hear from you. Select "Sponsorship Inquiry" in the topic dropdown and tell us about your brand. We'll follow up with our 2027 sponsorship package.
Can I volunteer at the event? ▾
Yes — volunteers are a huge part of what makes this event work. Select "Volunteering" as your topic and let us know your availability and what you'd like to help with.
I'm a journalist or content creator. Who do I contact? ▾
Select "Media / Press" as your topic. Include your outlet, what you're working on, and your timeline. We'll respond within 48 hours.
