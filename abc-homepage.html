What Is the ABC

Built Around the Whole Hunter

There's a certain kind of weekend that sneaks up on you. You show up expecting a contest — targets, scorecards, a podium — and somewhere between the first arrow and the last campfire laugh, it becomes something else entirely.

The Appalachian Bowhunter Challenge was designed to test more than your shot. It tests the body that carries the bow, the mind that holds steady under pressure, and the character that shows up when the mountain takes something from you.

Three stages. No shortcuts. No skipping the hard parts.

The Competition

Three Stages.
No Shortcuts.

Every element of this competition was designed to mirror the demands of a real backcountry hunt. The mountain doesn't care about your range score. The animals don't care how fast you think you can run.

01
🏋️

5K Weighted Pack Race

Competitors strap on weighted packs and cover five kilometers and nearly 1,000 feet of elevation gain. If you can't get past the gravel parking lot, the rest doesn't matter.

Fitness · Endurance
02
🎯

3D Course Shoot

A winding layout through mountain terrain. No rangefinders. Read the distance with your eyes, manage the angles, and make the shot — with everything already burning.

Skill · Precision
03
🏹

Head-to-Head Bracket

March Madness style. Sandbags. Sled drags. One-on-one eliminations. Then step up and make the shot — breathing hard, muscles torched, heart rate through the roof.

Composure · Character

These guys are as hardcore as it gets. They're obsessed just like I'm obsessed. It's nice to be able to reach out to somebody else that's as obsessed as you.

— Lucas Geiman, 2026 Competitor
