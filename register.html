What to Expect

01

5K Weighted Pack Race

Strap on a loaded pack and cover five kilometers of mountain terrain, gaining nearly 1,000 feet of elevation. Your pack race time feeds directly into your overall score — so show up fit.

02

3D Archery Course

A winding course through Winterplace's terrain. No rangefinders — read the distance with your eyes. Twelve targets, varied angles, and your legs still burning from Stage 1.

03

Head-to-Head Bracket

March Madness-style single elimination. Before each shot you'll complete a fitness challenge — sandbag carries, sled drags — then step up and make the arrow count. One bad shot ends your run.

04

Awards & Ceremony

Division winners and the Overall Grand Champion are recognized at the awards ceremony. Prizes from Mathews Archery, Flying Squirrel Forge, Tecumseh Taxidermy, and more.

Registration FAQ

What does registration include? ▾
Your entry fee covers all three stages of competition, a competitor packet, access to the awards ceremony, and — thanks to our sponsors — a full-year MTN TOUGH fitness platform membership for every registered competitor.
How many spots are available? ▾
The field is intentionally kept small to maintain the quality of competition and the camaraderie that makes this event what it is. The 2026 event had 50 competitors. Expect similar numbers in 2027 — register early.
What bow equipment is allowed? ▾
Any legal hunting bow is welcome. Compound, traditional (longbow/recurve), and crossbow all have dedicated divisions. Rangefinders are not permitted on the 3D course — this is intentional. The course is designed to be shot by eye.
What weight is required for the pack race? ▾
Pack weight requirements will be specified in your competitor packet and confirmed via RunReg closer to the event. Weight requirements may vary by division. Youth competitors will have an adjusted weight standard.
Is there lodging at Winterplace? ▾
Yes — Winterplace Ski Resort has on-site lodging options. We recommend booking early as availability fills up. Details and a lodging discount code will be included in your confirmation email after registering through RunReg.
What is the refund / cancellation policy? ▾
Refund and cancellation policies are managed through RunReg. Please review their policy at the time of registration. If you have extenuating circumstances, reach out to us via the Contact page and we'll do our best to work with you.
I have a question not answered here. ▾
Use our Contact page — select "Registration" as your topic and we'll get back to you within 48 hours. We read everything.
